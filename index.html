Welcome to Ayasya Yoga Mandira

Iyengar Yoga Therapy focuses on precision, alignment, and therapeutic application of yoga postures to improve physical, mental, and emotional well-being. It uses props such as belts, blocks, and blankets to help practitioners achieve correct alignment and gain therapeutic benefits tailored to individual needs, making it accessible and effective for people of all ages and abilities.
